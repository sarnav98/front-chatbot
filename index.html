    Heath Care Chatbot

    In the medical context, AI-powered chatbots can be used to triage patients and guide them to receive the appropriate help. Chatbots are considered a more reliable and accurate alternative to online searches patients carry out when they're trying to understand the cause of their symptoms.

    Benefits of Healthcare Chatbot

    AI chatbots can support clinical teams by lightening their daily caseloads. After analyzing the patient data, bots can suggest an online discussion with a clinician rather than a visit to their physical office. In its essence, the chatbot technology used in medical contexts promises to ease the burden on medical professionals.
